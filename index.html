Population Growth in India

Rapid Population Growth

India's population is growing at an unprecedented rate, surpassing 1.4 billion people. Urbanization, resource scarcity, and housing concerns are rising.

Urban Overcrowding

Metro cities are experiencing a strain on infrastructure and services. Traffic congestion, pollution, and unemployment are rising issues.

Education & Awareness

Promoting education, especially among women, is key to managing birth rates.

Healthcare Access

Improving reproductive health and access to contraception helps control growth.

Government Policies

Implementing effective population policies can lead to sustainable development.
